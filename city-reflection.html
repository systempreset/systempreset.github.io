City Reflection

The City of London is a historic heart where centuries-old streets and buildings stand side by side with towering glass and steel skyscrapers. These modern structures reflect and reframe the past, creating a striking visual dialogue. City Reflection is primarily a printmaking project that captures this unique juxtaposition.
